Underground Rap Scene

Underground rap has evolved significantly through platforms like SoundCloud, Discord, and niche online communities. Artists often prioritize experimentation, distorted production, and aggressive aesthetics over mainstream appeal.

Subgenres such as rage, pluggnb, and internet trap have gained traction among younger audiences, spreading rapidly through short-form content and online forums.

Artist Profile: Osamason

Osamason is associated with the newer wave of underground rap artists whose sound blends high-energy production with chaotic, abrasive delivery. His music reflects the DIY nature of modern underground scenes.

Rather than traditional promotion, artists like Osamason rely heavily on online presence, visual identity, and cult-like fanbases to build momentum.
